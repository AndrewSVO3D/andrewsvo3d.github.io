Overview

West Hollywood College Preparatory School (WHCP) is a private, nonprofit Pre-school through 12th grade center of learning located in the heart of West Hollywood, California.

At WHCP, we emphasize achievement in math and science and offer a stellar physics program. Students learn in a nurturing environment with small classes, enjoy a superb curriculum and faculty, and are encouraged to participate in civic and community life.

Principal's Message

🦉
School Principal
West Hollywood College Prep

Welcome to WHCP

Welcome to West Hollywood College Preparatory School. Our school stands as a beacon of educational excellence in the heart of West Hollywood, providing students from Pre-school through 12th grade with the tools, knowledge, and values they need to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

At WHCP, we believe that every child deserves a personalized education that honors their unique strengths and nurtures their curiosity. Our small class sizes are intentional. They allow our outstanding faculty to build genuine relationships with every student and tailor instruction to meet each learner where they are.

We place a special emphasis on mathematics and science, including a stellar physics program that sets our students apart. Our graduates consistently gain admission to top colleges and universities, and they carry with them not just academic preparation, but a deep sense of civic responsibility and community engagement.

I invite you to come visit our campus, meet our team, and see firsthand what makes WHCP such a special place to learn and grow. We are open for enrollment throughout the year and welcome families from all backgrounds.

Mission & Goals

The principles that guide every decision, every classroom, and every student's journey at WHCP.

Our Purpose

Mission Statement

"West Hollywood College Preparatory School is dedicated to preparing young minds for the future by providing an exceptional, personalized education in a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, civic responsibility, and lifelong learning."

🏆 Academic Excellence

We hold our students to the highest academic standards, with a rigorous curriculum that emphasizes critical thinking, problem-solving, and a deep love of learning, especially in math and sciences.

🤝 Personalized Learning

Small class sizes ensure that every student receives individualized attention. Our teachers know each student by name and tailor instruction to support their unique needs and aspirations.

🌆 Civic Engagement

We prepare students not just for college, but for life as engaged citizens. Students are encouraged to participate actively in the West Hollywood community and beyond.

🌱 Whole-Child Development

Academic success is only part of the picture. We nurture the emotional, social, and character development of every student, producing graduates who are thoughtful, compassionate, and resilient.

Strategic Goals

What We Strive For

1️⃣

College Readiness for Every Student

Ensure that every WHCP graduate is academically prepared and personally confident to succeed at a four-year university or the post-secondary path of their choice.

2️⃣

STEM Excellence

Maintain our reputation as a leader in math and science education, including our distinguished physics program, preparing students for careers in the 21st-century economy.

3️⃣

Inclusive, Welcoming Community

Foster a diverse, inclusive school environment where students from all backgrounds, including international students, feel welcomed, respected, and supported.

4️⃣

Family Partnership

Build strong partnerships with parents and families, keeping them informed, involved, and empowered as essential partners in their child's education.

Middle & High School

Grades 6–12. A rigorous, college-preparatory education with a distinctive emphasis on math, science, and physics.

College Preparatory Excellence

WHCP's Middle and High School program is designed to challenge and inspire students in grades 6 through 12. Our college-preparatory curriculum is built on high academic standards, small class sizes, and a faculty that genuinely cares about every student's success.

We place a particular emphasis on mathematics and the sciences. Our physics program is exceptional, and students regularly advance well beyond grade level in STEM disciplines, giving them a significant advantage when applying to top universities.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Advanced Mathematics through Calculus and beyond
  • Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with hands-on lab work
  • English Language Arts and Composition
  • History, Government, and Social Sciences
  • Foreign Languages
  • College Application Counseling beginning in 10th grade
  • SAT/ACT preparation integrated into curriculum

College Admissions Support

Our dedicated college counseling begins in 10th grade. We work closely with each student and family to identify the right colleges, craft compelling applications, and secure scholarships. Our graduates attend four-year universities across the country.

Small Class Sizes, Big Results

Our middle and high school classes are kept intentionally small. Every student is known by their teachers. Learning is personalized, expectations are high, and the results speak for themselves.

Kindergarten & Elementary School

Grades K–5. Building the foundation for a lifetime of learning in a warm, nurturing environment.

A Strong Foundation

Our Kindergarten and Elementary program gives students in grades K–5 the strong academic and social foundations they need to thrive. Our talented, caring teachers create classrooms where every child feels safe, seen, and eager to learn.

We understand that the early elementary years are formative. That's why we invest so heavily in small class sizes and individualized instruction, so that every child's needs are met, every curiosity is honored, and every student builds genuine confidence as a learner.

What We Teach

  • Reading, phonics, and writing foundations
  • Mathematics and early numeracy
  • Science exploration and inquiry
  • History and social studies
  • Arts, music, and physical education
  • Social-emotional learning and character development

The WHCP Difference

Elementary students at WHCP benefit from the same high standards and dedicated faculty that make our middle and high school program exceptional. As students progress, they are seamlessly prepared for the rigors of WHCP's college-prep upper school curriculum.

Early Childhood Education

Pre-school & Kindergarten. Play-based, developmentally rich learning that sparks a lifelong love of discovery.

Learning Through Play

WHCP's Early Childhood Education program provides a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for our youngest learners in Pre-school and Kindergarten. Our experienced early childhood educators understand child development deeply and design every activity to build both skills and joy.

We believe that play is the work of childhood. Through guided play, exploration, storytelling, music, and hands-on activities, our young students develop language, mathematical thinking, social skills, and creative expression, all while having fun.

Program Goals

  • Build language and early literacy foundations
  • Develop number sense and early math concepts
  • Foster curiosity, creativity, and a love of learning
  • Build social and emotional skills: sharing, empathy, cooperation
  • Prepare children seamlessly for Kindergarten and beyond

A Safe, Nurturing Space

Our early childhood classrooms are bright, welcoming, and designed with young learners in mind. We maintain very small group sizes so that every child receives individual attention and care. Our teachers are warm, patient, and deeply committed to each child's development.

After School Programs

Before & after school childcare and enrichment, a safe and engaging space for students beyond the school day.

Before & After School Care

WHCP's Before and After School program provides working families with reliable, high-quality care before and after the regular school day. Our program is staffed by caring WHCP educators who know the students, ensure safety, and make the extended day both productive and enjoyable.

Enrichment & Activities

After school is not just supervision. It's an extension of learning. Students participate in:

  • Supervised homework time and academic support
  • Enrichment clubs and activities
  • Arts and creative projects
  • Physical activity and recreation
  • STEM exploration activities
  • Reading and independent study time

Individual Programs

Fully customized, one-on-one learning designed around each student's unique needs, goals, and pace.

Learning Built Around You

WHCP's Individual Programs offer a completely personalized educational experience for students who need or prefer a one-on-one learning environment. Whether a student needs remedial support, is academically accelerated, or has unique learning needs, we build a custom program around them.

Who Benefits

  • Students needing to catch up in specific subjects
  • Advanced students ready to move beyond grade level
  • Students with learning differences who thrive one-on-one
  • Students recovering from illness or extended absence
  • Home-school students seeking structured supplemental instruction
  • Students preparing for high-stakes exams

How It Works

Each individual program begins with an assessment to understand the student's current level, learning style, and goals. We then design a custom curriculum, set benchmarks, and pair the student with the teacher who is the best match. Progress is reviewed regularly and the program adjusts as needed.

Tutoring Center

Expert subject tutoring for students of all ages and grade levels, with flexible scheduling to fit your family.

Expert Academic Support

WHCP's Tutoring Center provides targeted, expert academic support in all core subjects. Our tutors are credentialed teachers and subject-matter specialists who know how to identify gaps, build understanding, and restore student confidence.

Whether a student is struggling with a single concept or needs ongoing support in a subject, our tutors deliver clear, patient, and effective instruction at the student's pace.

Subjects We Cover

  • Mathematics (all levels, through Calculus)
  • Physics, Chemistry, Biology
  • English Language Arts and Writing
  • History and Social Studies
  • Foreign Languages
  • SAT, ACT, and standardized test preparation
  • Study skills and academic organization

Flexible Scheduling

We offer tutoring sessions after school, on weekends, and during school breaks. Sessions can be scheduled as needed or as recurring appointments. We work with your family's schedule to make tutoring accessible and consistent.

International Students

A welcoming home for students from around the world, with the dedicated support needed to thrive in U.S. education.

A Home Away From Home

WHCP proudly welcomes students from countries around the world. Our International Student Program provides the specialized support, resources, and community that international students need to succeed academically and feel at home in West Hollywood.

We understand that transitioning to a new country, language, and school system is challenging. Our dedicated international student coordinator works closely with each student and their family to ensure a smooth transition and ongoing success.

Program Support

  • English as a Second Language (ESL) instruction
  • Academic support and subject tutoring
  • Cultural orientation and adjustment support
  • I-20 and student visa documentation assistance
  • Family communication support
  • Housing referral resources (if needed)
  • Integration into the broader WHCP community

Los Angeles & West Hollywood

Students who attend WHCP get to experience life in one of the world's most vibrant, diverse cities. West Hollywood is a welcoming, culturally rich neighborhood and a wonderful home base for an international student's American experience.

We Serve

Students at all grade levels (Pre-school–12th grade) from any country are welcome.
